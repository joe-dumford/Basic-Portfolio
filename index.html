Brief History

As graduate from Georgia College & State University with a BA in Fine Arts, Joe has always gravitated towards the creative process. This is one of the aspects that drove him to enroll in Georgia Tech and pursue web based design. Since graduation from GC&SU, he worked for an advertisement agency and eventually found his way into the film and television industry. While here, he realized this was not his final career path. Upon his reassessment, he took the hard, but necessary, steps of leaving the industry to go to work for Starbucks to put himself through school. Joe believes persistent hard work, determination and the right mindset are the keys to success in any aspect of life. It's this core belief that has given him the confidence to pursue his goal of software development.
